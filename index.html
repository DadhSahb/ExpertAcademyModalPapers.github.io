11th Class Prep Guide BISE Abbottabad

10-Week Master Revision Calendar — FSc Pre-Engg, BISE Abbottabad

A single rolling schedule that covers all six subjects without burning students out. Assumes ~3 hours of study per day, 6 days a week.

How the week is structured

  • Mon / Wed / Fri: Sciences (Math · Physics · Chemistry) — heaviest cognitive load.
  • Tue / Thu: Languages & Compulsory (English · Urdu · Islamiyat) — memory + writing practice.
  • Sat: Mixed problem-solving + past-paper attempt.
  • Sun: Rest / catch-up only — no new chapters.

Tips for the tutor

  • Don't skip mock-marking sessions. Students learn more from analysing their own wrong answers than from any new lecture.
  • Review the last 3 years of past papers in Week 10. Patterns repeat — especially in derivations (Physics) and named reactions / numericals (Chemistry).
  • Force timed practice. A student who can solve 8 SRQs in 32 minutes at home but only 5 in the exam loses 12 marks to nothing but pacing.
  • Compulsory subjects need writing reps, not just reading. Make students physically write at least one full essay/letter per week.
